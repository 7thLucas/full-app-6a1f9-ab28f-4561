# Cafe Dash — Product Information

## Overview
Cafe Dash is a casual time-management simulation game where players take orders from customers, prepare food and drinks, and deliver them to the correct tables before time runs out.

## Core Gameplay Loop
1. **Order** — Customers arrive and place orders
2. **Prep** — Player prepares food and drinks
3. **Deliver** — Player delivers items to the correct tables
4. **Clear** — Table is cleared and ready for new customers

## Target Audience
- Casual mobile gamers
- Kids and family players
- Fans of Diner Dash and time-management games

## Brand Identity
- Name: Cafe Dash
- Tone: Fun, energetic, friendly, casual
- Feel: Colorful, approachable, lighthearted, fast-paced excitement

## Strategic Principles
- Accessibility first — simple controls, easy to pick up
- Rewarding progression — players feel accomplishment after each level
- Visual clarity — clear UI so players always know what to do next
- Casual-friendly — no punishing mechanics, encourages replaying

## Anti-References
- Avoid dark or gritty aesthetics
- Avoid overly complex UI or information overload
- Avoid anything that feels corporate or sterile
